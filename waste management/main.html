RENEWABLE ENERGY

We’re generating renewable natural gas from our own landfills to power our trucks or forging partnerships to turn closed landfill sites into solar fields, Waste Management is committed to finding new sources of energy.



MODERN LANDFILLS

The scene you picture when you hear the word landfill is a thing of the past. Today’s landfills are highly-engineered, safe and often able to serve another purpose after they’ve been closed.



RECYCLING

Waste Management was on the forefront of the recycling movement when we introduced single-stream recycling for our customers. And now we’re looking towards the future by investing in new ideas, new facilities and new markets.



ORGANICS RECYCLING

More than one-third of food goes uneaten around the globe so Waste Management is leading the charge in developing new technologies and finding new ways to process organic waste and turn it into valuable resources.
